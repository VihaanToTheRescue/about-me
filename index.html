About Me

Yo, I’m Vihaan, a 17-year-old dodging adulting like it’s a pop-up ad.
I code apps when my Wi-Fi isn’t throwing a tantrum, bike to save the planet (or outrun my to-do list), and study law to win arguments with traffic lights.
My brain’s 80% bad puns, 20% caffeine, and I survive on snacks and sheer chaos.
Fitness? I sprint to the fridge during ad breaks.
Social skills? I’m a face-to-face charmer and call-crushing pro, but chats and family functions turn me into a buffering robot—send pizza, stat.
I’m saving the environment one reusable straw at a time while pretending I’ve got life figured out.
Spoiler: I don’t, but I’m faking it with style.
